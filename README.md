## üìñ My Approach to Troubleshooting

**1. Reproduce the Issue**  
I recreate problems in my own environment to understand them fully before attempting fixes.

**2. Gather Evidence**  
Collect logs, screenshots, metrics‚Äîdocument everything before making changes.

**3. Systematic Diagnosis**  
Work methodically from most likely cause to least likely, testing one variable at a time.

**4. Document Everything**  
Every incident gets: problem statement, evidence, root cause, resolution steps, prevention.

**5. Learn & Improve**  
After fixing, I ask: "How can I prevent this?" and "What can I automate?"
